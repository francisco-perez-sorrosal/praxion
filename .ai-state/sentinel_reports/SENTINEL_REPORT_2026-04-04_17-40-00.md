## Ecosystem Health: C

### Summary

The ecosystem has grown to 29 skills, 12 agents, 8 rules, 15 commands, 68 reference files, 8 docs, 13 ADRs, and 15 hooks (up from 26 skills, 12 agents, 6 rules, 12 commands at the last audit on 2026-04-01). Three new skills (`context-security-review`, `testing-strategy`, `versioning`), three new commands (`full-security-scan`, `release`, `test`), two new rules (`memory-protocol`, `testing-conventions`), and significant hook infrastructure were added.

**Regression from last B grade**: The always-loaded token budget overshoot has worsened from 10,469 tokens (+23%) to 11,024 tokens (+30%) due to two new always-loaded rules (`memory-protocol.md` at 4,205 chars). README drift has also widened: `README.md` understates counts (says 26 skills / 12 commands, actual 29 / 15), `README_DEV.md` is missing 3 skills, 2 commands, 2 rules, 1 doc, and 9 hooks from its project structure tree.

**What is healthy**: Agent definitions are complete and consistent. Plugin.json agent paths all resolve. ADR system is fully consistent (13 files, all with valid frontmatter, index matches). All SKILL.md files have descriptions in frontmatter. All commands have description fields. Agent coordination protocol and agents/README.md tables match agent files 1:1. Self-verification passes on all four checks.
